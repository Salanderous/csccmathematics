\begin{exercise}

$\int e^x f(x) dx= e^x \int f(x) dx$
\begin{hint}
Take a simple function $f(x)=1$. 

Then we have
$\int e^x  dx= e^x \int 1 dx$

Compute the left  side of "equality".

$ \int e^x dx=e^x+C$

Compute the right  side of "equality".

$e^x \int1 dx=e^x\left(x+C\right)$
\end{hint}
