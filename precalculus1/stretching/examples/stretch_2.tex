Let $f(k) = \sin(k)$, be the basic sine function with its natural domain. \\


\begin{question}


Let $G(t) = f(8 - 5 t)$


Which type of transformation is associated with the $5$?


\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice {horizontal shift}
\choice {vertical shift}
\choice[correct] {horizontal stretch}
\choice {vertical stretch}
\end{multipleChoice}


\end{question}
