\begin{question} Shifted Domain 

Define $M(y) = P(y - 2)$ \\



The graph of $z = M(y)$ appears to be the graph of $y = P(t)$, just shifted \wordChoice{\choice{left} \choice[correct]{right}} by $\answer{2}$ units.


\end{question}
