\begin{question} Domain of $P$


What is the domain of $P$?

\[
\left( \answer{-8} , \answer{-3} \right]  \cup  \{ -2 \}  \cup  \left( \answer{0} , \answer{8} \right]
\]


\end{question}

\end{question}
