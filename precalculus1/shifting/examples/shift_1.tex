\begin{question} Shifted Graph 

Define $f(x) = V(x - 3)$ \\


The graph of $z = f(x)$ appears to be the graph of $y = V(h)$, just shifted \wordChoice{\choice{left} \choice[correct]{right}} by $\answer{3}$ units.

\end{question}















\begin{question} Shifted Domain 

Define $G(t) = V(t + 4)$ \\


The graph of $k = G(t)$ appears to be the graph of $y = V(h)$, just shifted \wordChoice{\choice[correct]{left} \choice{right}} by $\answer{4}$ units.

\end{question}
