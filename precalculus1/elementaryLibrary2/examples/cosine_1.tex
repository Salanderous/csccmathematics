\begin{question}


As you travel on the unit circle counterclockwise from $0$ radians to $\frac{\pi}{2}$ radians, you move up and to the left. \\


On the interval $\left (0, \frac{\pi}{2} \right)$, $\cos(\theta)$ is \wordChoice{\choice{increasing} \choice[correct]{decreasing}}. \\

\end{question}
