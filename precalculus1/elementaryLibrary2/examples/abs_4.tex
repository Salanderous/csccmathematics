\begin{example}











Rewrite $g(x) = | -3x + 6 |$ without absolute value bars.



\begin{explanation}


First, establish signs.

\begin{itemize}
\item $-3x + 6 < 0$ when $x > \answer{2}$
\item $-3x + 6 > 0$ when $x < \answer{2}$
\end{itemize}





Now, remove bars.

\[
A(x) = 
\begin{cases}
  \answer{-3x + 6} & \text{ on } \left( -\infty, \answer{2} \right)   \\
  \answer{-(-3x + 6)}  & \text{ on } \left[ \answer{2}, \infty \right).
\end{cases}
\]
